# Web Accessibility Matters

Building an accessible web is not just about compliance; it's about empathy and creating a better experience for everyone.

## Why it Matters
Over a billion people worldwide experience some form of disability. If your website isn't accessible, you're locking out a massive portion of the population. Furthermore, accessible design often translates to better usability for all users, including those on mobile devices or in challenging environments.

## Quick Wins
- Ensure all images have descriptive `alt` text.
- Maintain sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds.
- Make sure your website is fully navigable using only a keyboard.
