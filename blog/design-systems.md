# Building Scalable Design Systems

Design systems bridge the gap between design and development, ensuring consistency across your entire product ecosystem.

### Core Principles
1. **Design Tokens**: Centralize your variables (colors, typography, spacing) into tokens.
2. **Component Isolation**: Build components that are truly independent and reusable.
3. **Documentation**: A system is only as good as its documentation. Keep it clear, concise, and up to date.

By investing time in a solid design system, you accelerate development and guarantee a unified user experience.
